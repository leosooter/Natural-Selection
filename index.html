Letter Predator Plugin

Most websites have way too many letters.

Control the population of letters on your site by introducing letter predators.

    Why use Letter Predators?
  • Too many letters on a page can result in unhealthy overcrowding and monotonous blocks of text.
  • Reducing the number of letters yourself can be time consuming and may even require the use of expensive copy editors
  • Introducing letter predators on your site is an easy and humane way to reduce letter populations. Plus, its free!.
